# Product Overview + Technical Specs

## What this blueprint creates
A structured “Product Overview + Technical Specs” asset for GTM and buyer-facing communication.

## Designed for
- GTM operators
- product marketing
- sales enablement
- founders and product teams

## Best used when
- a product needs a clear overview plus technical detail in one asset
- teams need faster, consistent drafts for launch or enablement
- buyers or internal stakeholders need quick technical understanding

## How to use it
1. Collect the required inputs in `content.md`.
2. Provide intent and style guidance from `intent.md` and `style.md`.
3. Generate output using the structure in `output.md`.
4. Review and edit for factual accuracy and audience fit.

## Expected output
A concise, scannable document that explains what the product does, who it is for, why it matters, and the key technical specifications.

## Notes on customization
Adjust section depth, tone, and terminology to your audience (executive, technical buyer, partner, or internal enablement).
